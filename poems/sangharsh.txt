---translation---
May you always remember
that nothing is impossible for you.
Never step back from a challenge —
let struggle run in your very blood.

Obstacles will surely appear on your path;
be fully aware of that truth.
Even if you stumble at times,
let your self confidence remain firm.

Let hard work and patience
stand beside every effort you make.
May your fight to reach your goal
stay alive until the very end.

Success sprouts only from failure —
so let failure never become your regret.
And even when your goal seems far away,
let not a trace of ego enter your heart…
